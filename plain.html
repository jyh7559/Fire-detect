Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a team of telecommunications engineers, TowerTech Solutions emerged from a simple observation: tower management was fragmented, reactive, and inefficient. We set out to change that.

Over the past decade, we've grown from a small startup to the industry's most trusted tower management partner, serving over 500 clients across North America with our innovative solutions and dedicated support.

Today, we manage over 10,000 towers, preventing countless outages and optimizing performance for critical infrastructure that millions depend on daily.
